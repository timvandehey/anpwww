About Advance Name Plate & Badge
We are an independent engraving shop located in Waukesha, Wisconsin serving local and national customers alike since 1974. We pride ourselves in providing a quality product while maintaining great customer service. You can expect the fast and friendly service that only an independently owned and operated business can provide. No job is too small. Give us a call during normal business hours and a real live person will gladly accept your call. Plastic, wood, steel, stainless, and aluminum are just some of the materials we are able to handle. Please check the “Products” tab to view the vast array of items we offer that are manufactured in house as well as hundreds of others manufactured by our partners.
